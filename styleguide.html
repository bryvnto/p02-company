Style Guide

What does the company do, or offer?

This company will be a brewery. They will have a physical retail location where different types of beer will be available for purchase and consumption. In addition to beer, customers are able to order food to consume along with their beverages. Additional merchandise, such as hats and shirts, can be purchased by customers in-store or online. Using our website, customers will be able to find our contact information, reserve a table, look through our menu, and learn about our company.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

This company will be targeted to young adults above the legal drinking age. We will be heavily marketing towards young professionals heading to happy hour after work. Simultaniously, we hope to attract environmentalists and humanists due to our ethical sourcing and vegan ingredients.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

The ingredients we use to brew our beer are ethically sourced and vegan. Our in-store location will intice customers to take images for sharing purposes on social media.

Personas

Jonathan Gosling

Occupation: Sous Chef

Age: 25

Behavioural Patterns:
Jonathan works in the nearby restaurant and is always looking for a new place to spend time with his friends.

Personality:
Jonathan is a care-free person. He only cares if the food and drinks are the way he wants them. He likes being the one to make the reservations or order the food, so his friends let him lead them wherever he wants them to go.

Eva Leung

Occupation: Medical Resident

Age: 28

Behavioural Patterns:
Eva works at a hospital in the downtown core but lives all the way in the suburbs. She often travels by public transit and passes by Riley Park every day. After a long day at work, she often goes out with her co-workers for drinks before she passes out at home.

Personality:
Eva cares about the environment. She has recently started to live a more zero-waste lifestyle and has been looking for new ways to help the environment.

Oscar Nunez

Occupation: Undergraduate Student/Instagram Influencer

Age: 21

Behavioural Patterns:
Oscar lives in Riley Park. He often walks past the shop on his way to the bus stop. He wants to find a new place to hang out with his friends that is not as nosy as the bars down the street. Oscar also studies environmental science, so he is familiar with the impacts that our society has on our environment. Oscar is an Instagram influencer, therefore he is always on the look out for a new spot for his posts.

Personality:
Oscar is a laid-back person. He is really tired of heading to the clubs and is looking for something more mature and relaxed.

About

Our Story

During a stroll in their childhood neighbourhood, siblings Benjamin and Isabelle Johnston spotted a large retail space for lease in the epicentre of Riley Park, and they knew what they wanted to do. They envisioned a space between the pretentious bars and the young nightclubs for the people of Riley Park and thus, the Riley Park Brewing Company was born.

It was the perfect balance that Riley Park needed. The once sleepy neighbourhood became more lively as more retailers and restaurants moved in as a result of the brewery's success.
