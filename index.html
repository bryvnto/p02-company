Our Story

During a stroll in their childhood neighbourhood, siblings Benjamin and Isabelle Johnston spotted a large retail space for lease in the epicentre of Riley Park, and they knew what they wanted to do. They envisioned a space between the pretentious bars and the young nightclubs for the neighbourhood to enjoy a whole new brewing experience, and thus, Live Kombucha Brewing Co. was made.

It was the perfect balance that Riley Park needed. The once sleepy neighbourhood became more lively as more retailers and restaurants moved in as a result of the brewery's success.

Our Location

Situated in the heart of the Riley Park neighbourhood on the fascinating South Main strip of Vancouver, Live Kombucha Brewing Company is truely in the heart of the city, connecting East to West and North to South.
