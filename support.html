Our Location

Situated in the heart of the Riley Park neighbourhood on the fascinating South Main strip of Vancouver, Live Kombucha Brewing Company is truely in the heart of the city, connecting East to West and North to South.

By Public Transit

Our location is easily accessible by the 25 bus that connects to the King Edward station on the Canada Line and the 3 Main bus that connects to the Main Street-Science World station on the Expo Line.

By Cycling

Bike racks are available at our front door. Let our staff know if you require any additional space for your bicycles.

By Car

Metered street parking is available.
